Learn More About EQNOIR

EQNOIR is a decentralized movement designed to uplift Black and LGBTQIA+ communities by providing ownership, access, and empowerment through blockchain. This isn’t just a coin — it’s a call to action for cultural wealth and liberation.

With EQNOIR, we build bridges to technology, art, and equity — blending resistance with innovation. Your investment and support means direct participation in a system that finally centers us.
