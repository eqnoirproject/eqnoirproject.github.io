What is EQNOIR?

EQNOIR is a decentralized movement built to empower Black and LGBTQIA+ communities through technology, culture, and ownership. By leveraging blockchain, we are creating financial equity, cultural sovereignty, and new pathways to generational wealth. EQNOIR isn’t just a token — it’s a tool for liberation, governance, art, and innovation. We believe in a future where inclusion isn’t a feature — it’s the foundation.
