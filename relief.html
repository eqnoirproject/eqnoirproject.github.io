Altadena Relief Fund

In response to the devastating fires affecting the historic Black community of Altadena, EQNOIR is dedicating part of its Friends & Community Reserve to direct relief efforts.

We're setting aside 100 Billion EQNOIR to support affected families, partner organizations, and rebuild initiatives in Altadena, CA.

🚨 Why Altadena?

Altadena is a predominantly Black neighborhood rich in culture, legacy, and generational wealth. Sadly, it’s being overlooked by major aid efforts. EQNOIR stands to change that.
