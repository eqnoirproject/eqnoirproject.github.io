About EQNOIR

EQNOIR is a revolutionary decentralized finance (DeFi) platform designed to empower underserved Black and LGBTQIA+ communities globally. By leveraging cutting-edge blockchain technology, EQNOIR creates transparent, secure, and inclusive financial tools that redefine access to wealth and opportunity.

Our mission is to bridge financial gaps and provide equitable access to investment opportunities, fostering long-term community growth and prosperity.
