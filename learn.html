What is EQNOIR?

EQNOIR is a decentralized cultural revolution. Designed to uplift the Black and LGBTQIA+ communities, it fuses future-forward blockchain technology with deep cultural roots. Through EQNOIR, we create equity, ownership, and innovation — unapologetically rooted in brilliance, resilience, and pride.

This is not just crypto — it’s a cultural currency. A digital liberation tool for communities long excluded from wealth-building platforms. EQNOIR is for the dreamers, the changemakers, and those who see themselves in a tech-powered future.

Built by us. Governed by us. Owned by us. Join the movement shaping a decentralized tomorrow where inclusion leads, art speaks, and collective brilliance thrives.
